# Specialized Agents

Forecast AI uses specialized agents:
- **News Agent**: Geopolitical & political news.
- **Social Agent**: Sentiment velocity.
- **Reddit Agent**: Community discussion trends.
- **Research Agent**: Academic papers and scientific data.
- **Macro Agent**: Macroeconomics & monetary policy.
- **Onchain Agent**: Transactions & whale behaviors.
- **Market Agent**: Orderbooks & price dynamics.
